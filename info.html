Shanzhai is culture aims to break stigmas around bootleg practices, particularly in China, and encourage shanzhai practice as a tool to inform design practices at large.

This site is resource dedicated to the shanzhai phenomenon. Housing a user uploaded gallery and vast library of texts that discuss shanzhai and adjacent practices.
